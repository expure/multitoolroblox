## ✨ Key Features (Unique to EXVS)

*   **🛡️ Collide Mode** – The most **convenient** movement method. Just move – it works. No complex settings, no weird behaviour. Pure, smooth, reliable speed.
*   **🎮 Grabber** – True telekinesis. Grab players and objects with a spring-loaded connection and drag them wherever you want.
*   **💥 Puncher** – Click any object to launch it into the stratosphere with extreme physics (handles mass up to 3 million).
*   **⚓ Move Stabilization** – Absolute immunity to being flung, grabbed, or moved without your input. Works perfectly in `Fling Things and Players`.

## 🎮 Supported Games (Optimized Configs Available)

This is a **universal** script. However, separate, optimized **lightweight versions** are available for specific games to give you the best performance and a simplified UI.

*   **Universal Mode** – Full script with all 9 speed methods, tools, and menus. Works in *any* game.
*   **Strongman Simulator** – Optimized script focusing on **Strength Mode** (pull/push heavy objects) and **Grabber**.
*   **Fling Things and Players (FTaP)** – Optimized script focusing on **Move Stabilization** (anti-fling) and high-speed **CFrame** movement.
*   *(More game-specific configs coming soon)*

## ⚙️ All Features

### 🎯 Movement
*   **9 Speed Methods:** Off, BodyVelocity, WalkSpeed, CFrame, **Collide**, **Legit**, Tween, Velocity, **Strength** (pull/push heavy objects)
*   **Fly Mode:** WASD + camera control with independent speed slider (0.001x – 1500x)
*   **Adaptive Legit:** Acceleration of 600 (or 150) studs/s² depending on the game.

### 🛡️ Defense & Control
*   **Move Stabilization:** Blocks all external forces (flinging, grabbing, server teleports).
*   **Y-Stabilizer:** Counteracts unintended upward impulses at high speeds.
*   **Noclip:** Walk through walls.
*   **Fling:** Ragdoll and launch other players.

### 🤖 Visuals & Aiming
*   **ESP:** Player highlights and beams.
*   **AimBot:** Hold RMB to snap camera to the nearest player.
*   **FullBright:** Permanent maximum brightness.

### 🕹️ Utilities
*   **Grabber (Tool):** Pick up and hold any unanchored object or player.
*   **Puncher:** Click any object to launch it with extreme force.
*   **Click Teleport:** Teleport to mouse cursor.
*   **Tween Move:** Smooth, animated movement to cursor position.
*   **Infinite Jump:** Unlimited jumping.

### 📊 GUI & Customization
*   Resizable and draggable menu.
*   Logariphmic speed slider (0.001x – 8,000,000,000x).
*   Notifications for all actions.
